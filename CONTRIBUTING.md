# Contributing

memex is open source, but closed to contributions: external pull requests
aren't reviewed or accepted, and a workflow closes them automatically. This
keeps the project small and lets it move at its own pace — please don't take
a closed PR personally.

Bug reports and ideas are very welcome as
[issues](https://github.com/kocieusz/memex/issues). And per the
[license](LICENSE), you're of course free to fork.
